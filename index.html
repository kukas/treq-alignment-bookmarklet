Try it out!

The bookmarklet is intended for use with Treq.

  1. On Treq, select a supported language pair such as Czech-English.
  2. Write a query, for example "pes" and click on one of the English equivalents (for example: dog)
  3. Now you are in Kontext and are ready to use the bookmarklet.
  4. Click on the "Treq word alignment" bookmark in your bookmark toolbar when having the Kontext window open.
  5. Now move your mouse over some of the words in the results, the alignment should appear as yellow highlights.

The supported language pairs are:

  • cs-uk
  • cs-en
  • en-et

The bookmarklet supports both language pair directions.
